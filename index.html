about me

Hi there! Glad you stumbled on my little corner of the internet. My name is Robert- but my friends call me Robbie. I'm a 17-year-old student based in Bellingham, WA. I consider myself a bit of a "Jack of all trades," and like to dabble and excel in many different fields. I'm a full-stack developer, engineer, machinist, mechanic, filmmaker, a/v technician, director, and broadcast engineer, just to name a few. I'm a technical-minded individual with a passion for creating and building things. I Enjoy being pragmatic in my work, with some room for creativity on top. I'm always looking for new projects to work on, so if you have something in mind, feel free to reach out!
